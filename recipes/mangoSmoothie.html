Mango Smoothie Recipe

Description:

The Club Sandwich is a great way to enjoy lunch while socialzing. A simple and easy recipe, to mass produce for all to enjoy. I hope that you can easily follow this recipe, and even add your own little changes to spice it up!

Ingredients:

  • 6 slices white or whole wheat bread, toasted
  • 1/2 cup mayonaise
  • 2 tablesppons Dijon mustard
  • 4 thin slices roast turkey or chicken breast
  • 8 lettuce leaves
  • 8 slides bacon, cooked until crisp
  • 1 tomato, thinly sliced
  • 2 thin slices red onion
  • 4 basil leaves, torn into pieces (optional)

Steps:

  1. Place toasted bread on a cutting board.
  2. Whist together mayonaise and mustard and spread on each piece of toast.
  3. Top 2 pieces of toast with 2 slices of turkey.
  4. Top each with a lettuce leaf, then 2 pieces of bacon and 1 slice of tomato
  5. Top each with another slice of toast, mayonaise side down
  6. Spread more mayonaise mixture on top of toast, then layer with remaining turkey, lettuce, bacon, and tomato.
  7. Top with red onion, basil leaves, and remaining slices of toast, and press down gently.
  8. Cut each sandwich in half and serve
  9. Don't forget to enjoy!
